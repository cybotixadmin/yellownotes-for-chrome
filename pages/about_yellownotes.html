Yellownotes

We want to place yellow sticky notes on the internet.

One of the most brilliant inventions in document-based communication was the post-it note from 3M.

Very good. use this bit!
Gone where the days of scribbling guiltily in the margins. With post-its you could jot down a quick note and stick it on the document.' regardless of what the document was. Comments and instructions could be placed where they where needed.

Inital here

Yellownotes seeks to replicate this functionality on the web.

Plus a few extra pieces of functionality. Ever found a lose Post-it note under your desk. And thrown it away beacause you had no way of finding out where it belonged ? Or worse, kept it because it had something important looking written on it. Yellownotes have a "go-home" button. Click it and you are taken to where the note was attached - With the note rendered in place.

Tired of receiving links to articles and documents where you have neither the time nor the inclination to wade through all of it to get to the relevant bit? If you send or receive a yellownote attached to the web page in question, you can read the remarks on the note and click to go-home button to get taken to the exact location. And if the note was attached to a selection of the document text, that text will be highlighted.

The present version of Yellownotes

The notes come in two types. The "classic" and the webframe.

The classic is simply a yellow square that can be placed anywhere on the page and be adjusted in size and shape. It can anchored to a piece of text or be placed on the same xy-coordinates on the page as when it was saved.
